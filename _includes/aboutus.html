About Us

We are upset about the lack of mobile games that offer tangible educational value. Current offerings are either boring or focus solely on memorization. We have created Bubblo World Preschool as a first step towards addressing these problems for preschool kids. Bubblo World Preschool helps kids develop their problem solving skills offering them needed intelligence and brain cognitive development. We understand that screen time should be limited and that's why it's critical to play this game together with your kids.
